The idea is to click somewhere and it will act as a grappling hook, with the object swinging around the page. The reality is far more...wiggly.

This is less of a game and more of a proof of concept. I wanted to see if I could make a rudementary physics sim for kinematic motion. The results are... interesting so far. Have fun with the complete nonsense I've somehow created. I may expand on this project for the final, its been interesting so far.
